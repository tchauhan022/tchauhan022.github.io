About the work

Philosophy & Approach

I work at the intersection of IT delivery discipline and data-driven decision-making — structured execution paired with the analytics that make it visible.

Six-plus years across enterprise IT delivery, program governance, and business analytics. I've scaled from leading distributed offshore teams on a $2B+ insurance platform program at TCS to PMO leadership across 13 university departments. I lead through clear frameworks, not force of personality: integrated project plans, RAID logs, RACI matrices, Agile ceremonies, Power BI dashboards, and the kind of documentation that survives team turnover.

My edge is the handoff between delivery execution and reporting intelligence. I don't just track projects — I build the reporting infrastructure that lets leadership see risk, momentum, and milestone health in real time. From standardizing 90+ SOPs and governing release schedules at TCS to automating dashboard refreshes that cut reporting effort by 30%+ at ISU, the pattern is consistent: reduce noise, increase signal, deliver on commitment.

I'm especially drawn to environments where IT delivery is complex, dependencies span systems, and continuous improvement isn't a buzzword but a daily habit.
