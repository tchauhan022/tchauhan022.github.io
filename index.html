About the work

Six-plus years across enterprise operations, delivery governance, supply chain coordination, and business analytics. I've scaled from high-volume insurance processing at TCS to PMO leadership across 13 university departments. 04 / Case Studies

Case studies

Three engagements, in focus
Flagship · TCS

Stabilizing delivery inside a $2B+ insurance TPA program.

Transamerica · 2018 to 2023 · Onshore / Offshore

Tata Consultancy Services' largest contract at signing: a multi-year Third-Party Administration mandate to digitize and administer over 10M life, annuity, and supplemental health policies. As Project Lead, my job was to keep delivery steady across distributed teams while the program absorbed a platform migration and an annual cost-savings target north of $70M.

The work spanned 15+ parallel operational streams. Each had its own SLA, defect profile, and upstream/downstream dependencies. The difference between an on-time delivery and a client escalation often came down to 48-hour triage: reprioritize the backlog, reallocate resources, add a quality checkpoint, communicate up. When the system migrated to TCS BaNCS, QA coordination and release validation had to hold while volume continued to run.
